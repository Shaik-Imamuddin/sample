If the violation occurs we have to perform rotation or recoloring

cases when violation occurs


null nodes in a red black tree always considered as black.

case-1:
    if the uncle is red

    in this case we perform recoloring. 

        recolor parent to black
        recolor uncle to black
        recolor grand parent to red.

        If the grandparent becomes the root, recolor it to black.
            Otherwise, check for further violations.

case-2:
    uncle is black and zig zag order

    structure:
        LR or RL

        if LR perform left rotation.
        if RL perform right rotation.


        in this case will make a rotation

        perform rotation on parent
            this makes the tree linear like case(3).

case-3:
    uncle is black and Linear order 

    structure:
        LL or RR

        if LL perform right rotation.
        if RR perform left rotation.

        in this case will make a rotation 

        perform rotation on grandparent
        perform recoloring.
